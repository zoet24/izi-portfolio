Izi thexton
Projects

Izi Thexton's practice does not revolve around a single medium, but adapts to find appropriate, concept-driven ones. Obsessed with the material qualities of physical objects, her projects span from publication to weaving to blacksmithing and more.

These analogue processes of hands-on making (particularly in textiles) further her material understanding and generate ideas. However, what underpins Izi's practice is an aim to highlight and facilitate discourse around a need for change.

She uses her stance of designer-as-publisher not to speak for people, but to bring lesser-heard voices to the fore and challenge ingrained attitudes. She believes that using the design process as a form of research is enriched by collaboration and a sense of play.
